What it is

A thin, honest layer between intent and checkout.

Most shopping tools want to own your attention: a catalog to browse, a feed to scroll, data to collect. magicMCP does none of that. It stores no product database and tracks no profile of you.

You and your assistant shop the open web as usual. When a product link belongs to a merchant magicMCP supports, it returns that link in a form that helps fund the service — with no change to your price and no change to what gets recommended.

Connect

Add magicMCP in under a minute.

It runs as a remote connector, so there is nothing to install on your machine.

  1. Open your assistant's settings and find Connectors.
  2. Choose Add custom connector.
  3. Paste the magicMCP endpoint below and confirm.
https://magicmcp.xyz/mcp

On first connection you'll see a short note explaining that purchase links are affiliate links. That disclosure appears once — it never clutters your conversations.
