What it is

A thin, honest layer between intent and checkout.

Most shopping tools want to own your attention: a catalog to browse, a feed to scroll, data to collect. magicMCP does none of that. It stores no product database and tracks no profile of you.

You and your assistant shop the open web as usual. When a product link belongs to a merchant magicMCP supports, it returns that link in a form that helps fund the service — with no change to your price and no change to what gets recommended.

How it works

Three steps, then it stays out of the way.

  1. 01

    You shop normally

    Ask your assistant for help. It searches the web and finds real products, the same way it always has.

  2. 02

    magicMCP recognizes the merchant

    When a product link points to a supported store, the connector identifies it instantly from memory — no scraping, no waiting.

  3. 03

    You get the right link

    It returns a purchase link that supports magicMCP at no extra cost to you. If the store isn't supported, you simply get the original link.

Principles

Built to be trusted, not just used.

Same price, always

An affiliate link never costs you more. The price at checkout is identical to buying direct.

Recommendations aren't for sale

What your assistant suggests is based on fit, not commission. Monetization happens after the choice is made.

Nothing to store about you

No accounts, no profiles, no product catalog. The only state magicMCP keeps is its list of supported merchants.

Connect

Add magicMCP in under a minute.

It runs as a remote connector, so there is nothing to install on your machine.

  1. Open your assistant's settings and find Connectors.
  2. Choose Add custom connector.
  3. Paste the magicMCP endpoint below and confirm.
https://magicmcp.xyz/mcp

On first connection you'll see a short note explaining that purchase links are affiliate links. That disclosure appears once — it never clutters your conversations.
